The timeline follows the planned delivery order.
Grey shadow
represents planned delivery time, with a ±15-minute window.
Blue dot
represents effective delivery time.
You can click anywhere on the timeline to directly control the animation and adjust its progress.
To view detailed information about the delivery, click on the color block on the map.
Spatial Distance Chart
X: Delivery Sequence.
Y: Deviation Level - shows the spatial distance between the planned and the actual delivery locations for the same order.
High Deviation (>1000m)
Medium Deviation (500~1000m)
Low Deviation (200m~500m)
On Track (<200m)
The graph provide a visual way to compare at each order, how far the postman was from where he was supposed to be. The goal is to have as many deliveries as possible "On Track" or with "Small Deviation."
